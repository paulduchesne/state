# Paul Duchesne

Paul Duchesne is a film archivist and software developer, based in Canberra. He has worked for the [NFSA](https://paulduchesne.github.io/personal-premis/resource/e5e95fdb-dda9-4c40-9273-395fe34aba3c) and [TIB](https://paulduchesne.github.io/personal-premis/resource/b8744ea7-c182-47c2-bf31-37e6ed6df396), and is a member of the [FIAF]() [Cataloguing and Documentation Commission]().
